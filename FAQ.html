    *How do I become a member of the Library? 

    -IIIT-NR faculty, students, and staff are bona-fide members and no other formality is required.
        New Member details will be added in the Library database once your academic registration is done.


     *How do I borrow books? 
 
    -Go to the library, pick up the books you like to borrow, carry these books to the Circulation Counter.
       The library staff will assist you in getting these books issued to you. 

    *How/where I pay my library dues? 

    -Library dues (late fees/cost of lost books etc) can be paid in cash at the Library Cash Counter. 


    *How many books I can issue?

    -B.Tech students:- 5 Books 

    -Faculty Members:- 15 Books 


   *what is the fine if re-issued of book is detailed? 
 
   -one rupee for per day per book will be charged.
